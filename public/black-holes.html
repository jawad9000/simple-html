5 Facts About Black Holes

  1. Black holes are regions in space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape.
  2. They are formed when massive stars collapse under their own gravity at the end of their life cycle.
  3. The point of no return around a black hole is called the event horizon.
  4. Supermassive black holes, with millions or billions of times the mass of the Sun, exist at the centers of most galaxies.
  5. Black holes can be detected by observing their effects on nearby matter, like the orbits of stars or the emission of X-rays.
