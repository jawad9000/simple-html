5 Facts About Gravitational Waves

  1. Gravitational waves are ripples in space-time caused by accelerating massive objects.
  2. The first direct detection of gravitational waves occurred in September 2015 by LIGO.
  3. Gravitational waves carry crucial information about cosmic events like black hole mergers.
  4. The effect of gravitational waves on Earth is extremely small, often less than 1 part in 10^21.
  5. Gravitational wave astronomy is a new field that helps study phenomena like dark matter and the early universe.
